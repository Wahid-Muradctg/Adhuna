About Us

Adhuna Accessories Ltd. is a manufacturer and supplier of garments accessories and services, proudly serving the domestic and international markets from Bangladesh. Our mission is to be the indispensable helping hand for garments manufacturers by delivering the best quality and most rapid supply chain solutions.

We began our journey in 2014 with a commitment to innovation and quality, starting with a humble team of just 5 employees. Through dedication and a fresh, innovative approach to supply, we have grown exponentially and are now a robust company of 100+ highly skilled professionals.

Our growth reflects our commitment to excellence, allowing us to expand beyond the domestic market. We are now a trusted export partner, supplying our high-quality garment accessories to clients who export their goods around the globe, primarily serving the demanding markets of North America, Europe and Asia. We also export directly to Ethiopia, Uzbekistan, El Salvador and South Korea.
